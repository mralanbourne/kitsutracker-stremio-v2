  • Automatic (Watch Progress & Anime Lists): Whenever you watch an episode or update your lists on Kitsu, Stremio syncs this automatically in the background (visible after 5 minutes due to caching). No reinstall needed!
  • Manual (Catalog Changes): If you change which lists are visible below (e.g., hiding "Dropped"), Stremio requires you to uninstall the old addon and click "Install" again.
